ListVIS

ListVIS is a way of visualizing contents of a CSV file. Every column of the CSV file is considered as a “list” and the visualization represents connections between the individual members of a list. Connections can be both internal and external. VISLists summarizes all papers and authors from the IEEE VIS Conferences (VAST, InfoVis, SciVis).
